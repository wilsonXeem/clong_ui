The Burden behind CloNG Project Int'l

In April 2014, during my internship as a Pharmacist, the Lord placed on me the burden for the next generation leadership. It was borne out of prayer and passion to see men and women that will be clothed with the PRINCIPLES AND THE PRESENCE of God. The next generation of leaders that will stand out on the mountain of education and beyond shall be men that know HIS PRINCIPLES and are robed with HIS PRESENCE.

Three years later, on April 19th, 2017, at the place of prayer, the Lord showed me a nest of golden eggs without a mother hen. It got me bewildered as to why there will be nest of eggs but no mother hen to incubate them. As I pondered on the vulnerability of these golden eggs to waste and destruction, I saw flood light from Heaven shine upon the nest. Then He said to me, THE GOLDEN EGGS represent the NEXT GENERATION OF LEADERS, THE NEST signifies CloNG-Project platform while THE LIGHT from Heaven is THE SUPERNATURAL PRESENCE and PRINCIPLES OF GOD.

CloNG project is the nest that will nurture and provide a platform for the LIGHT of God's Presence and Principles to incubate the Next generation of Leaders that will excellently represent God in all fields of endeavour.

In January 2018, the vision was officially launched while our first conference held in February, 2022.
