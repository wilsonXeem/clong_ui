Our Story

CloNG Project was founded with a divine vision to bridge the gap between academic excellence and spiritual growth. What started as a small initiative to support deserving students has grown into a comprehensive platform that nurtures Godly leaders across various fields.

Over the past decade, we have witnessed countless testimonies of transformation - from scholarship recipients who have become industry leaders to conference attendees who have discovered their divine purpose in their careers.
