Who We Are

From our Chicago base, we mentor students one-on-one and in cohorts, preparing them for college and graduate school and equipping them to thrive as change-makers and apostles in the marketplace.

We are committed to bridging opportunity gaps by providing graduate school preparation, academic mentorship, leadership development, and career guidance for youth from diverse economic backgrounds all over the world. Our goal is that every young person we encounter will be spiritually grounded and academically empowered.

We are passionately vested in restoring and equipping the next generation of leaders, empowering them to shine with God's light and represent Him with excellence in their respective callings.

Our Mission

To restore, transform, and preserve the next generation of leaders that are carriers of LIGHT and excellently represent God globally in the academia and marketplace.
