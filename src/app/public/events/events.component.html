2025

Ambidextrous Generation Conference 2025

September 25-27, 2025

Theme: Royal Priesthood: The Rise of Priestly Kings and Kingly Priests

This online conference inspired participants to embrace their dual callings of spiritual devotion and leadership excellence. It ignited renewed purpose, birthed mentorship networks, and empowered attendees to lead with divine authority in their careers, ministries, and communities. The conference marked a significant wave of transformation, unity, and kingdom-driven impact.

2024

Ambidextrous Generation Conference 2024 (#AGC2024)

September 5-6, 2024

Theme: The Pursuit of Purpose

Special Event: First Chicago Prophetic Prayer Walk

CloNG's flagship annual event. This hybrid conference (live in Chicago and online) convened students and mentors to "pursue purpose" in both God's kingdom and career fields. Highlights included worship services, motivational keynotes, and expert panels. Notably, a session on "Securing Funded Graduate Programs in the United States" demystified scholarship and fellowship opportunities for attendees. The conference featured industry and academic speakers, networking fairs, and prayer gatherings, reinforcing CloNG's vision that "ministry is impact" beyond the pulpit.

2023

Ambidextrous Generation Conference 2023 (#AGC2023)

Theme: Ambidextrous Generation

Format: Virtual Conference

Our second edition expanded to include workshops on leadership and apostolic ministry. Delegates learned about real-life examples of faith-led enterprise and community transformation. Pharm. Peculiar Onyekere (CloNG founder) taught that a Christian's calling extends to every vocation. Alumni who had previously attended shared testimonies of CloNG project impact in their lives.

2022

First Conference of CloNG Project International - "Sons of Light Conference"

February 2022

Note: Now renamed as "Ambidextrous Generation Conference"

The inaugural conference of CloNG Project International, originally tagged "Sons of Light Conference." This historic first conference laid the foundation for what would become the annual Ambidextrous Generation Conference series. The event brought together young leaders, mentors, and visionaries to launch CloNG's mission of raising Godly leaders in academia and career.

2020

Prophetic Prayer Walk in the University Campus

2020

Location: University Campus

The 2020 edition of the Prophetic Prayer Walk brought together students and believers for a powerful spiritual mobilization across the university campus. This faith-driven initiative combined prayer, worship, and prophetic declarations, creating a spiritual atmosphere for transformation and revival within the academic community.

2019

Community Outreach to The Place of Hope Orphanage Nsukka

January 2019

Occasion: President's Birthday Celebration

A heartwarming community outreach to The Place of Hope Orphanage in Nsukka, organized in celebration of the President's birthday. This event demonstrated CloNG Project's commitment to serving the most vulnerable in society, bringing joy, support, and care to orphaned children while embodying the organization's values of service and compassion.

2018

Official Inauguration Ceremony of Clothing The Next Generation Project Intl

January 2018

Venue: Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences, University of Nigeria Nsukka

The historic official inauguration ceremony marking the formal launch of CloNG Project Intl. This milestone event at the University of Nigeria Nsukka established the foundation for the organization's mission to raise Godly leaders in academia and career. The ceremony brought together faculty, students, and community leaders to witness the birth of this transformative initiative.
